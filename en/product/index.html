Product

The dashboard for choosing, delegating, and verifying across AI models.

LogK is organized around the real user workflow for multi-model AI: select the stack, estimate the cost, dispatch across providers, then compare or verify the outcome.

Select Choose one model or many

Pick a single model for speed, or a multi-model stack for comparison, aggregation, and task-specific coverage.

Estimate See expected credits before run

Make price visible up front so users can decide whether broader delegation is worth it for the task.

Delegate Dispatch across providers

Send the request through one system while LogK handles provider choice, central tracking, and workflow structure.

Verify Follow up when needed

Trigger verifier passes, compare outputs, and produce one trusted final answer instead of stopping at first response.

Workspace

A real workspace flow instead of an abstract demo

Model choice, execution controls, privacy blocking, and spend estimation all read as one product surface.

Pricing

Usage and spend presented as product UI

Pricing should feel like an operating surface, not a detached spreadsheet, so credit-based billing becomes easier to trust.

Surface 01

Model board for intelligent selection

LogK turns model choice into a clear product surface with cards, presets, recommendations, and spend visibility, rather than forcing users to remember vendor-specific behavior.

Surface 02

Cost-aware delegation

Credit estimates and centralized billing make it practical to fan out across multiple models without losing visibility into spend.

Surface 03

Answer aggregation and disagreement handling

LogK compares outputs, identifies disagreement, and lets users choose between direct comparison or aggregated synthesis.

Surface 04

Privacy and policy controls

Sensitive inputs can be blocked, redacted, or routed according to policy before they ever leave the product.

Flow

A product story that users understand in seconds.

Strong startup sites show the workflow clearly. LogK should communicate the loop quickly: choose models, see price, delegate, verify, decide.

01
Write the question once

02
Select the model stack

03

Preview credits, apply privacy blocking, and dispatch through one coordinated system.

04
Inspect and verify the result

Compare outputs, run follow-up verification, and finish with one answer you can actually trust.
